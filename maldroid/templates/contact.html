Welcoem to the Android Malicious Application Scanner, or malDroid. This utility is designed to scan potentially malicious .APK files, in the aid of frensic analysis of malicious applications of executables found on Android mobile devices. This is an Open Source project for the Mobile Security course at NYU: Polytechnic School of Engineering, Fall 2014.
